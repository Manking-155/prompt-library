# SOP: Financial Management for Solo Developer Business

## Overview

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the systematic process for managing all aspects of financial operations in a solo developer business, including revenue tracking, expense management, tax compliance, and financial planning.

## Prerequisites

- Vietnamese bank account(s) for business transactions
- Digital payment processing accounts (PayPal, Stripe)
- Accounting software or tracking system
- Tax identification number (TIN) in Vietnam
- Business registration certificates
- Understanding of Vietnamese tax regulations
- Financial tracking templates and spreadsheets

### 4. Tax Management

#### 4.1 Vietnamese Tax System Overview
**Key Taxes for Solo Developer**:
```markdown
## Vietnamese Tax Framework

### Personal Income Tax (PIT)
- **Progressive Rates**: 5% to 35%
- **Standard Deduction**: 11 million VND/month (132 million VND/year)
- **Dependent Deduction**: 4.4 million VND/dependent/year
- **Filing Frequency**: Annual (quarterly option if >10 million VND/month)
- **Payment Due**: Annual filing by March 31st of following year

### Value Added Tax (VAT)
- **Standard Rate**: 10%
- **Registration Threshold**: 100 million VND/year
- **Filing Frequency**: Monthly or quarterly
- **Deductible Input Tax**: Available for business expenses
- **Payment Due**: 20th of following month

### Business License Tax
- **Rate**: Varies by industry (typically 1-2 million VND/year)
- **Payment Due**: January 31st each year
- **Based on**: Charter capital
- **Registration**: Required annually

### Digital Services Tax (for international clients)
- **Google/Facebook Ads**: 5% on gross revenue
- **Apple App Store**: No direct tax
- **Google Play Store**: No direct tax
- **PayPal**: No direct tax on income

### Double Taxation Agreements
- **Vietnam has treaties with**: 80+ countries
- **Benefits**: Reduced withholding taxes
- **Documentation**: Required for tax exemption
```

## Tax Compliance Calendar

### Monthly Obligations
- **5th**: Previous month financial report completed
- **15th**: VAT payment due (if monthly filing)
- **20th**: VAT filing deadline (if monthly)
- **20th**: PIT payment due (if >10M VND/month)

### Quarterly Obligations
- **20th**: VAT filing deadline (if quarterly)
- **Last day**: Quarterly tax payment due

### Annual Obligations
- **March 31**: Annual PIT filing deadline
- **January 31**: Business license tax due
- **March 31**: Annual financial statements (if applicable)

## Best Practices

1. **Record Everything**: Keep detailed records of all income and expenses
2. **Separate Business and Personal**: Maintain separate bank accounts and records
3. **Stay Current**: File taxes on time and keep up with regulations
4. **Plan Ahead**: Set aside money for taxes and expenses
5. **Review Regularly**: Analyze financial performance monthly
6. **Get Professional Help**: Consult tax professionals when needed
7. **Maintain Documentation**: Keep receipts and supporting documents
8. **Use Technology**: Leverage accounting software and tools
9. **Monitor Cash Flow**: Keep track of cash in and out
10. **Plan for Growth**: Budget for expansion and investment

This SOP provides a comprehensive framework for managing the financial aspects of a solo developer business in Vietnam, ensuring compliance with local regulations while maximizing financial efficiency and profitability.
