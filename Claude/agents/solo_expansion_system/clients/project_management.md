# Client Project Management System

## 🎯 Client Service Strategy

### Service Offerings Portfolio

#### 1. AI-Powered Mobile App Development
**Target Clients**: Vietnamese SMEs, startups, international companies
**Pricing**: $5,000 - $25,000 per project
**Timeline**: 6-16 weeks depending on complexity

**Service Packages:**
- **Starter Package** ($5,000): Basic Flutter app with AI features
- **Professional Package** ($12,000): Full-featured app with backend
- **Enterprise Package** ($25,000): Complex app with custom AI integration

#### 2. Database Design & Backend Development
**Target Clients**: Companies needing scalable data solutions
**Pricing**: $80-120/hour or $3,000-8,000 fixed price
**Expertise**: MySQL, Oracle Cloud, Python APIs

#### 3. AI Integration Consulting
**Target Clients**: Businesses wanting to implement AI
**Pricing**: $150/hour consulting + implementation fees
**Services**: Strategy, implementation, training

## 💰 Pricing & Contract Management

### Pricing Strategy

#### Hourly Rates (By Service Type)
- **Flutter Development**: $60-80/hour
- **Backend Development**: $70-90/hour  
- **AI Integration**: $80-120/hour
- **Database Design**: $70-100/hour
- **Technical Consulting**: $100-150/hour

#### Contract Templates
**Key Contract Sections:**
1. **Scope of Work**: Detailed feature specifications
2. **Timeline**: Milestones với specific deliverables
3. **Payment Terms**: Schedule và late payment penalties
4. **IP Ownership**: Client owns code, developer retains methodology
5. **Change Requests**: Process và pricing for scope changes
6. **Support**: Post-launch support duration và terms
