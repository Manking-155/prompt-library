# TASK: 30-DAY SOCIAL MEDIA CAMPAIGN

## OBJECTIVE

Develop and deliver a comprehensive 30-day social media campaign strategy that drives measurable business results through a combination of organic content and paid advertising across relevant platforms.

---

## CONTEXT

### When to Use This Task
- Product/service launch campaigns
- Brand awareness initiatives
- Seasonal promotions or events
- Re-engagement campaigns
- New market entry
- Specific business goals with 30-day timeframe

### What This Deliverable Includes
1. Campaign strategy document
2. Detailed 30-day content calendar
3. Paid advertising plan with budget allocation
4. Creative guidelines and content briefs
5. KPI framework and measurement plan
6. Implementation timeline and checklist

### 2. 30-Day Content Calendar

**Format:** Spreadsheet/Table

**Columns:**
- Date (Day 1-30)
- Platform (FB, IG, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Content Type (Reel, Story, Post, Video, Carousel, etc.)
- Topic/Theme
- Copy/Caption (first draft or bullet points)
- Visual Description (what image/video needed)
- Hashtags
- Link/CTA
- Target KPI (engagement, traffic, conversions)
- Status (Planned/In Progress/Complete)
- Notes

**Content Distribution Guidelines:**
- Mix of formats (not all static images)
- Balance of content pillars (educational, entertaining, promotional)
- Strategic posting times
- Platform-specific optimization
- Sufficient variety to prevent fatigue

**Minimum Content Requirements:**
- Instagram: 4-7 posts/week (mix of Feed, Reels, Stories)
- Facebook: 3-5 posts/week
- TikTok: 3-5 videos/week
- LinkedIn: 2-3 posts/week (if B2B)

### 3. Paid Advertising Plan

**Campaign Structure Document**

**Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)**

**Campaign Hierarchy:**
```
Campaign 1: [Campaign Name] - Awareness
├── Ad Set 1.1: Prospecting - Interest Targeting
│   ├── Ad 1.1.1: [Creative Concept]
│   ├── Ad 1.1.2: [Creative Concept]
│   └── Ad 1.1.3: [Creative Concept]
└── Ad Set 1.2: Prospecting - Lookalike Audience
    └── [Ads 1.2.1-1.2.3]

Campaign 2: [Campaign Name] - Consideration
└── Ad Set 2.1: Engagement Retargeting
    └── [Ads 2.1.1-2.1.3]

Campaign 3: [Campaign Name] - Conversion
├── Ad Set 3.1: Website Visitors Retargeting
└── Ad Set 3.2: Add-to-Cart Retargeting
```

**For Each Ad Set, Specify:**
- Objective (Awareness, Traffic, Engagement, Conversions)
- Audience definition (interests, behaviors, demographics)
- Budget (daily or lifetime)
- Placement strategy (automatic or manual)
- Schedule (always on or scheduled)

**Creative Strategy:**
- 3-5 ad variations per ad set (test different hooks, CTAs)
- Format mix (image, video, carousel)
- Platform-specific adaptations
- A/B testing plan

**Budget Allocation Example:**
- Total Budget: $5,000
  - Prospecting (Awareness): $2,000 (40%)
  - Consideration: $1,500 (30%)
  - Conversion: $1,500 (30%)

**TikTok Ads** (if applicable)
- Campaign structure
- Targeting strategy
- Creative requirements (native format)
- Budget allocation

**Google Ads** (if applicable)
- Search campaigns for high-intent keywords
- Display retargeting
- YouTube ads (if video content available)

**Optimization Plan:**
- Daily: Budget pacing, critical issues
- Every 3 days: Creative performance, audience adjustments
- Weekly: Comprehensive review, strategic pivots

### 5. KPI Framework & Measurement Plan

**KPI Dashboard Structure**

**Overall Campaign Metrics**
- Total Reach (unique users)
- Total Impressions
- Total Engagement (likes, comments, shares, saves)
- Engagement Rate %
- Total Website Clicks
- Total Conversions (leads/sales)
- Cost per Result
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) - if e-commerce

**Platform-Specific KPIs**

**Instagram:**
- Follower growth (net new followers)
- Feed post engagement rate
- Reels views and plays
- Reels engagement rate
- Story reach and completion rate
- Profile visits
- Link clicks (Stories/Bio)

**Facebook:**
- Page likes growth
- Post reach (organic + paid)
- Engagement rate
- Video views (3-second, 10-second, ThruPlay)
- Link clicks
- Conversion events

**TikTok:**
- Follower growth
- Video views
- Average watch time %
- Engagement rate
- Profile visits
- Link clicks

**LinkedIn** (if B2B):
- Follower growth
- Post impressions
- Engagement rate
- Click-through rate
- Lead generation (if applicable)

**Paid Advertising KPIs**
- Total Spend vs. Budget
- CPM (Cost per 1000 Impressions)
- CPC (Cost per Click)
- CTR (Click-Through Rate)
- CPA (Cost per Acquisition)
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
- Frequency (average impressions per user)

**Conversion Funnel Metrics**
- Top of Funnel: Reach, Impressions
- Middle of Funnel: Engagement, Website Visits
- Bottom of Funnel: Leads, Sales, ROAS

**Reporting Schedule:**
- **Daily**: Quick performance check (traffic, spend pacing)
- **Weekly**: Summary report with key metrics and insights
- **End of Campaign**: Comprehensive final report

**Success Criteria Example:**
- Primary Goal: Generate 500 leads
- Secondary Goals:
  - Grow Instagram followers by 2,000
  - Achieve 5% engagement rate
  - Maintain CPA below $25
  - Achieve 4x ROAS (if e-commerce)

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

The campaign is considered successful if:

**Must Have:**
- [ ] Primary business objective achieved (or >80% of goal)
- [ ] Campaign launched on time without major issues
- [ ] Budget spent efficiently (not overspent or grossly underspent)
- [ ] No brand reputation issues or crises
- [ ] Positive ROI or acceptable CPA relative to customer LTV

**Should Have:**
- [ ] All secondary KPIs met or exceeded
- [ ] Engagement rates above industry benchmarks
- [ ] Audience growth targets achieved
- [ ] Content performance insights documented for future use
- [ ] Creative assets reusable for future campaigns

**Nice to Have:**
- [ ] Viral content moment (1M+ organic reach)
- [ ] User-generated content captured for future use
- [ ] Media/press coverage or influencer mentions
- [ ] Exceeded goals by 20%+
- [ ] Positive brand sentiment increase measurable

## COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

**Strategy Issues:**
- ❌ Trying to do too much (focus is better than breadth)
- ❌ Ignoring existing audience (don't only chase new)
- ❌ No differentiation from competitors
- ❌ Unrealistic goals given budget and timeline

**Content Issues:**
- ❌ Over-promotional content (90/10 rule: 90% value, 10% promo)
- ❌ Generic stock photos (authentic > perfect)
- ❌ Inconsistent posting (hurts algorithm performance)
- ❌ No variety (all images or all text)
- ❌ Not adapting content for each platform

**Paid Advertising Issues:**
- ❌ Insufficient budget for learning phase (need $20/day min per ad set)
- ❌ Too many ad sets (spreads budget thin)
- ❌ Creative fatigue (frequency >4 means refresh needed)
- ❌ Ignoring retargeting (highest ROI audience)
- ❌ Setting and forgetting (requires daily monitoring)

**Measurement Issues:**
- ❌ Vanity metrics only (likes don't equal business results)
- ❌ No attribution setup (can't track conversions)
- ❌ Not tracking at all (flying blind)
- ❌ Comparing apples to oranges (different time periods, seasonality)

## EXAMPLES & TEMPLATES

### Example Campaign Concept

**Brand:** Sustainable Fashion E-commerce
**Objective:** Drive $50K in sales over 30 days
**Budget:** $5,000 ad spend
**Platforms:** Instagram & Facebook

**Campaign Theme:** "30 Days of Sustainable Style"
- Each day showcases one outfit styled different ways
- UGC from customers styling their pieces
- Behind-the-scenes of sustainable production
- Educational content on fast fashion impact
- Limited-time daily deals

**Content Pillars:**
1. Style Inspiration (40%): Outfit ideas, styling tips
2. Sustainability Education (30%): Why sustainable fashion matters
3. Behind-the-Scenes (20%): Production, team, materials
4. Promotional (10%): Sales, new arrivals, bundles

### Example KPI Targets

**30-Day Goals:**
- Revenue: $50,000 (5x ROAS on $10K total budget)
- New Customers: 500
- Instagram Followers: +3,000
- Engagement Rate: 4%+
- Website Traffic: 25,000 visitors from social
- Email Signups: 1,000

**Weekly Breakdown:**
- Week 1: Focus on awareness (reach, impressions)
- Week 2: Drive engagement (comments, saves, shares)
- Week 3: Convert traffic (website visits, add-to-carts)
- Week 4: Close sales (purchase conversions, upsells)

## NOTES FOR EXECUTION

**Flexibility:**
- While this is a 30-day plan, allow 20% flexibility for real-time opportunities
- Reserve 20% of content slots for trending topics or timely content
- Have backup content ready in case approvals delayed

**Team Coordination:**
- Daily standup (15 min) to align on priorities
- Weekly review meeting (1 hour) to assess and optimize
- Clear approval workflows (who approves what and when)
- Emergency contact protocol for issues

**Continuous Optimization:**
- Don't wait until campaign ends to optimize
- Make data-driven adjustments every 3-5 days
- Be willing to pivot if strategy not working
- Document all changes and rationale

**Post-Campaign:**
- Conduct thorough post-mortem
- Document learnings (what worked, what didn't, why)
- Archive all assets for future use
- Calculate final ROI and present to stakeholders
- Use insights to improve next campaign
