# TASK: PAID ADS CAMPAIGN SETUP & OPTIMIZATION

## OBJECTIVE

Design, set up, launch, and continuously optimize paid advertising campaigns across Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads, and/or TikTok that achieve specific business goals within defined budget and timeline constraints.

---

## CONTEXT

### When to Use This Task
- New product/service launches requiring immediate visibility
- Scaling existing campaigns with proven ROI
- Lead generation initiatives
- E-commerce sales campaigns
- Brand awareness with measurable outcomes
- Retargeting and conversion optimization
- Testing new audiences or markets

### What This Deliverable Includes
1. Campaign strategy and structure document
2. Complete account setup (if new)
3. Campaign build in platform(s)
4. Creative specifications and briefs
5. Tracking and conversion setup
6. Launch checklist
7. Optimization playbook

### 4. Creative Specifications & Briefs

**Creative Requirements by Platform**

**Meta (Facebook & Instagram):**

**Image Ads:**
- Dimensions: 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (vertical)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Max file size: 30MB
- Text: <20% of image (recommendation, not hard rule now)
- Resolution: High quality, no pixelation

**Video Ads:**
- Dimensions: 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1920px (Stories/Reels)
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Length: 15-30 seconds optimal (max 240 min)
- File size: Max 4GB
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (Feed), 4:5 (Feed optimized), 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
- Captions: Essential (85% watch without sound)

**Carousel Ads:**
- 2-10 cards
- Each card: 1080x1080px
- Different headlines and links per card (optional)

**Copy Guidelines:**
- Primary text: 125 characters for optimal display
- Headline: 40 characters
- Description: 30 characters (link description)
- Include emoji when appropriate ✅
- Clear call-to-action

**Google Ads:**

**Search Ads (Responsive):**
- Headlines: 15 (3-15 options, each 30 characters max)
- Descriptions: 4 (2-4 options, each 90 characters max)
- Display path: 15 characters per field (2 fields)
- Final URL with tracking parameters

**Display Ads (Responsive):**
- Images: Multiple sizes
  - Square: 1200x1200px
  - Landscape: 1200x628px
- Logo: 1200x1200px (square), 1200x300px (landscape)
- Headlines: 5 options (30 characters each)
- Descriptions: 5 options (90 characters each)
- Business name: 25 characters

**YouTube Ads:**
- Skippable In-Stream: 12 seconds - 3 minutes
- Non-Skippable: 15 seconds
- Bumper: 6 seconds
- Resolution: 1080p minimum
- Format: MP4, MOV, AVI

**TikTok Ads:**
- Video: 9:16 (vertical), 540x960px minimum, 1080x1920px recommended
- Duration: 9-15 seconds optimal (max 60 seconds)
- File size: Max 500MB
- Format: MP4, MOV, MPEG, 3GP, AVI
- Hook: First 3 seconds critical
- Captions: Recommended

### 5. Optimization Playbook

**Daily Monitoring Checklist**

**Every Morning (15 minutes):**
- [ ] Check spend pacing (on track for daily budget?)
- [ ] Review overnight performance (any issues?)
- [ ] Check ad delivery status (all ads running?)
- [ ] Monitor frequency (any ad sets >4?)
- [ ] Review comments and messages (respond if needed)

**Performance Thresholds:**

**Pause Immediately If:**
- CPA >2x target for 3+ days
- Frequency >5 with declining performance
- Ad rejected or flagged
- Budget overspend
- Negative engagement (many angry reactions, negative comments)

**Optimize Every 3-5 Days:**

**Creative Optimization:**
- Review creative performance (sort by CPA/ROAS)
- Pause bottom 20% performers
- Duplicate top performers with minor variations
- Refresh creative if frequency >3.5
- Test new hooks/angles

**Audience Optimization:**
- Analyze audience segments (which converting best?)
- Narrow underperforming audiences
- Expand winning audiences (lookalikes, broader interests)
- Add negative audiences (exclude non-converters)

**Bid & Budget Optimization:**
- Increase budget 20% on winning ad sets (if CPA stable)
- Decrease budget 30-50% on underperformers (before pausing)
- Adjust bid cap if needed (raise if limited by bid, lower if overspending)
- Reallocate budget from poor to strong performers

**Placement Optimization:**
- Review placement breakdown
- Pause low-performing placements (if manual placement)
- Note which placements drive best results

**Weekly Deep Dive (1-2 hours):**

**Analyze Data:**
- Export performance data
- Create pivot tables by:
  - Campaign
  - Ad set (audience)
  - Ad creative
  - Day of week
  - Time of day
  - Placement
  - Device (mobile vs. desktop)
  - Demographics (age, gender)

**Identify Patterns:**
- What's working? (double down)
- What's not? (fix or stop)
- What's surprising? (investigate)
- What's missing? (test)

**Strategic Decisions:**
- Continue, optimize, or pause campaigns?
- Need new creative? (request from team)
- Need to adjust strategy?
- Budget reallocation needed?
- New audiences to test?

**Scaling Playbook:**

**When to Scale (All Must Be True):**
- ✅ CPA at or below target consistently (7+ days)
- ✅ Frequency <3
- ✅ Conversion rate stable or improving
- ✅ Enough conversion volume (50+ conversions)
- ✅ ROAS positive and sustainable

**How to Scale:**
1. **Vertical Scaling**: Increase budget on winning ad sets
   - Increase by 20% every 3-4 days
   - Monitor for 24-48 hours after each increase
   - If performance degrades, reduce back 10%

2. **Horizontal Scaling**: Add more ad sets
   - Duplicate winning ad set with new audience
   - Create lookalikes from best customers
   - Expand interests to similar but untested

3. **Creative Scaling**: More ad variations
   - Test new hooks with same core message
   - Try different formats (image to video)
   - Seasonal or timely variations

**Troubleshooting Guide:**

**High CPA / Low ROAS:**
- Possible causes:
  - Audience too broad or wrong fit
  - Landing page not converting
  - Creative not compelling
  - Bid too low (not competing)
  - Too much competition (timing issue)
- Solutions:
  - Narrow audience or test different segment
  - A/B test landing page variations
  - Refresh creative with stronger hook/offer
  - Increase bid cap gradually
  - Adjust schedule to less competitive times

**Low Impressions / Not Delivering:**
- Possible causes:
  - Budget too low (<$20/day per ad set)
  - Audience too narrow (<50,000 reach)
  - Bid too low
  - Creative quality low (poor relevance score)
  - Account restrictions or policy issues
- Solutions:
  - Increase daily budget to $30+/day
  - Broaden audience (combine interests, expand age range)
  - Switch to automatic bidding initially
  - Improve creative (test new variations)
  - Check ad review status, appeal if rejected

**High Frequency, Low Performance:**
- Possible causes:
  - Audience size too small
  - Creative fatigue (seen too many times)
  - Not rotating new creative
- Solutions:
  - Expand audience size
  - Launch 3-5 new creative variations immediately
  - Set up creative rotation schedule (refresh every 2 weeks)

## COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

**Setup Mistakes:**
- ❌ Too many ad sets (spreads budget thin, prevents learning)
- ❌ Audience too narrow (<50K on Meta)
- ❌ Budget too low per ad set (<$20/day Meta, <$50/day Google)
- ❌ Forgetting to exclude converters from prospecting
- ❌ Not setting up conversion tracking properly

**Creative Mistakes:**
- ❌ Text-heavy images (hard to read on mobile)
- ❌ No captions on video (most watch without sound)
- ❌ Generic stock photos (stand out or scroll past)
- ❌ Weak hook (first 3 seconds don't grab attention)
- ❌ Unclear CTA (what should they do?)

**Optimization Mistakes:**
- ❌ Making changes too quickly (need 3-7 days of data)
- ❌ Changing too many things at once (can't isolate what worked)
- ❌ Ignoring frequency (creative fatigue kills performance)
- ❌ Not testing new creative regularly
- ❌ Scaling too aggressively (increase budget slowly)

**Measurement Mistakes:**
- ❌ Only looking at platform metrics (check Google Analytics too)
- ❌ Not accounting for attribution delay (conversions reported later)
- ❌ Comparing different time periods (weekday vs weekend)
- ❌ Ignoring assisted conversions (multi-touch attribution)
