# Business Validation Workflow

Quy trình có cấu trúc để validate business ideas và technical solutions.

## 🎯 Business Idea Validation Process

### Stage 1: Initial Concept Assessment (30 minutes)

#### Market Opportunity Evaluation
```markdown
## Idea: [Business Concept Name]

### Problem Definition
- **Target Problem**: [Specific problem being solved]
- **Affected Users**: [Demographics and psychographics]
- **Problem Severity**: [Scale 1-10 with justification]
- **Current Solutions**: [Existing alternatives]
- **Solution Gap**: [What's missing in current market]

### Market Size Assessment
- **Total Addressable Market**: [Global/regional size]
- **Vietnamese Market Size**: [Local opportunity]
- **Target Market Segment**: [Specific niche]
- **Growth Potential**: [Trend analysis]

### Competitive Landscape
- **Direct Competitors**: [Similar solutions]
- **Indirect Competitors**: [Alternative approaches]
- **Competitive Advantages**: [Unique differentiators]
- **Barriers to Entry**: [Market protection factors]
```

```markdown
### Technical Requirements
- **Core Technology Stack**: [Required technologies]
- **Development Complexity**: [Scale 1-10]
- **Integration Challenges**: [Third-party dependencies]
- **Scalability Considerations**: [Growth technical requirements]

### Resource Assessment
- **Development Time**: [Estimated timeline]
- **Required Skills**: [Technical capabilities needed]

### Risk Analysis
```

## 🎯 Decision Making Framework

### Validation Criteria Scoring
```markdown
## Validation Scorecard

### Market Opportunity (Weight: 30%)
- Market Size: [1-10 score]
- Growth Potential: [1-10 score]
- Competition Level: [1-10 score]
- Cultural Fit: [1-10 score]

### Technical Feasibility (Weight: 25%)
- Development Complexity: [1-10 score]
- Resource Requirements: [1-10 score]
- Technology Risk: [1-10 score]
- Scalability: [1-10 score]

### Business Viability (Weight: 25%)
- Revenue Potential: [1-10 score]
- Cost Structure: [1-10 score]
- Competitive Advantage: [1-10 score]
- Monetization Clarity: [1-10 score]

### Personal Fit (Weight: 20%)
- Skill Alignment: [1-10 score]
- Interest Level: [1-10 score]
- Time Availability: [1-10 score]
- Strategic Alignment: [1-10 score]

### Overall Score: [Weighted average]
### Decision: [Go/No-Go/Pivot]
```

### Go/No-Go Criteria
- **Score > 7.5**: Strong Go - High priority development
- **Score 6.0-7.5**: Conditional Go - Optimize weak areas first
- **Score 4.0-6.0**: Major Pivot - Significant changes needed
- **Score < 4.0**: No-Go - Focus on other opportunities
