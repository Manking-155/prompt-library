# Client Project Template

## Overview

This template provides a comprehensive framework for managing client projects from initial contact to final delivery, ensuring professional service delivery and client satisfaction.

## Project Lifecycle Framework

### Project Stages Overview
```
Client Project Lifecycle
├── Stage 1: Discovery & Scoping (Days 1-7)
├── Stage 2: Proposal & Contract (Days 8-14)
├── Stage 3: Project Kickoff (Days 15-21)
├── Stage 4: Development & Implementation (Weeks 4-12)
├── Stage 5: Testing & QA (Weeks 12-14)
├── Stage 6: Deployment & Launch (Week 15)
├── Stage 7: Handover & Training (Week 16)
├── Stage 8: Post-Launch Support (Days 17-30)
└── Stage 9: Project Closure (Day 30+)
```

## Stage 1: Discovery & Scoping (Days 1-7)

### Initial Contact Script Template
```markdown
## Initial Client Contact Template

### Introduction
"Hello [Client Name], thank you for reaching out regarding [project type]. My name is [Your Name], and I specialize in developing custom mobile and AI solutions for Vietnamese businesses."

### Information Gathering
Key questions to ask:
1. Business Context
   - Can you tell me about your business and industry?
   - What problem are you trying to solve?
   - Who is your target audience?
   - What are your business goals for this project?

2. Technical Requirements
   - What platforms do you need (iOS, Android, Web)?
   - Are there any specific technologies you prefer?
   - Do you have existing systems to integrate with?
   - What are your performance requirements?

3. Timeline & Budget
   - What is your target timeline?
   - Do you have a budget range in mind?
   - Are there any critical deadlines?
   - Is this your first software project?

4. Decision Making
   - Who will be the primary point of contact?
   - Who makes the final decisions?
   - Are there other stakeholders involved?
   - What is your decision timeline?

### Next Steps
"Based on our discussion, I'll prepare a detailed proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and investment. Would you be available for a follow-up call this [Day] to review it?"
```

## Stage 4: Development & Implementation (Weeks 4-12)

### Development Workflow Template
```markdown
# Development Workflow

## Sprint Structure (2-week sprints)
```
Week 1: Development
├── Monday: Sprint planning & task breakdown
├── Tuesday: Core feature development
├── Wednesday: Feature development continued
├── Thursday: Feature development & integration
├── Friday: Code review & weekly update
├── Saturday/Sunday: Optional work/catch-up

Week 2: Testing & Refinement
├── Monday: Feature completion & testing
├── Tuesday: Bug fixes and refinements
├── Wednesday: Integration testing
├── Thursday: Client review & feedback
├── Friday: Feedback implementation
└── Saturday/Sunday: Documentation and planning

```

├── Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
