#### Contract Templates

**Key Contract Sections:**
1. **Scope of Work**: Detailed feature specifications
2. **Timeline**: Milestones with specific deliverables
3. **Payment Terms**: Schedule and late payment penalties
4. **IP Ownership**: Client owns code, developer retains methodology
5. **Change Requests**: Process and pricing for scope changes
6. **Support**: Post-launch support duration and terms
7. **Termination**: Conditions and payment for work completed
